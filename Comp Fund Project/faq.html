Frequently Asked Questions

What are your opening hours?
We are open Monday-Friday, 7am -5pm. You can also contact us by telephone on +44 12345 67890, the same opening hours apply.

What do I need to open an account?
To open an account please fill out the form on our 'Contact Us' page and we will arrange an appointment in one of our branches to set up your account. For the appointment we will need a form of proof of identity (passport or driving license), proof of address (bank statement or bill) and your National Insurance Number.

What is the difference between a debit card and a credit card?
A debit card allows you to spend money directly from your bank account that you have put into the account while a credit card allows you to spend money that you have borrowed from the bank within a certain limit, the money has to be paid back within a specified time.

How do I apply for a loan?
Apply for a loan through the form on our 'Contact Us' page. after you have filled the form we will contact you to arrange an appointment in one of our branches. We will require proof of identity (passport or driving license), proof of address (bank statement or bill), proof of income and your credit score.
